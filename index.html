Advanced Methods

Energy-selective neutron imaging

Traditionally: Full beam spectrum used: high flux, short exposure times

But:

  • Cross-section is energy-dependent
  • Sharp Bragg edges: coherent elastic scattering up to 2dhkl=λ

Beamline lay-out

Diffraction effects in transmission: Bragg edges

Contrast from crystallographic properties

Energy resolving option required: velocity selector, crystal-based, time-of-flight
